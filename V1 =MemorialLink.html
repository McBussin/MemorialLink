Who are we?

MemorialLink provides a meaningful way to remember and honor the lives of loved ones who have passed away. Our platform uses QR codes to create virtual memorials that can be accessed anytime, anywhere, allowing family and friends to share memories, stories, photos, and videos in a private and secure space. With a user-friendly interface and customizable features, our online memorials offer a personalized and intimate tribute that celebrates the unique life and legacy of each individual. By embracing technology and the power of digital connectivity, MemorialLink seeks to provide comfort and solace to those who have experienced loss, while also preserving the memory and impact of those who have passed away. discord link
